Algebra

Algebra is a part of mathematics dealing with symbols and the rules
for manipulating those symbols. In elementary algebra, those symbols
(wich are written as letters) represent quantities withought fixed
values, these are known as variables.

Example:

1 + a = 2
What is a?



But algebra isnt alwase that easy, you can get more complex equations
like

5a - 20 = (10 / 2)

To solve this you need to eliminate everything other than the algebraic
symbols in the equation, so using that last question as an example you need
to simplify the equation to just leave the 5a. This is how:

5a - 20 = (10 / 2) ↓ Simplify

5a - 20 = 5 ↓ Add 20 to both sides

5a = 25 ↓ Devide by 5 on each side

a = 5

And thats algebra sometimes there is more that one symbol like a and b
but they work like this

2a + 10 = b + 10 ↓ Take away 10 from both sides

2a = b ↓ Devide both side by 2

a = b/2

Sometimes in algebra they dont give perfect answeres, as displayed on top.

Remember to simplify and alwase whatever
you do to one side do to the other.

Good Luck.
